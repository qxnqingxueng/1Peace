What is the maximum BWI aid amount for a flooded household, and what documents are required?
✦
According to NADMA Circular No. 3/2024, the Bantuan Wang Ihsan (BWI) flood relief provides:

• RM1,000 per affected household (basic tier)
• RM2,000 for severe structural damage (requires photo evidence)
• RM500 additional for households with OKU members

Required documents: MyKad (household head), utility bill matching registered address, disaster photos, and a JPS flood report number for the affected area.
📄 NADMA Circular 3/2024 §4.2 📄 JKM Aid Guidelines 2024 §7 📄 eKasih Registration SOP

What is the SOP for Pusat Pemindahan Sementara (PPS) activation?
✦
Per NADMA SOP Banjir 2023 §6.1, PPS activation is triggered when JPS water level readings reach Alert Level 2 (Amaran). District Officer (DO) issues activation order within 2 hours. Each PPS must have:

• Minimum 3 APM/RELA personnel on duty
• Basic necessities for 72-hour self-sufficiency
• Medical station (St John Ambulance or Red Crescent)
📄 NADMA SOP Banjir 2023 §6.1 📄 Penang State DRO Manual 2022
