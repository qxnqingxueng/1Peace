🤖 Section 4A — What it really means

Starting June 2024, the government is replacing the blanket diesel subsidy with a targeted system.


✅ Who still gets the subsidy: Registered fishermen, farmers, public transport operators, and households earning below RM5,000/month.


❌ Who no longer qualifies: Private car owners above a certain income threshold, as well as businesses not listed under the approved sectors.


📅 When: The change takes effect 1 October 2024 — but eligible citizens can register via MySejahtera until 30 Sept.

System Integration

How Everything Connects

The three layers — RAG retrieval, LLM generation, and Gemini agent execution — work in sequence, with your profile as the linking thread.

User profile is the single source of truth — set once, used everywhere. The RAG engine retrieves only the clauses relevant to your query (e.g. diesel subsidy + Selangor). The LLM generates a personalized summary and the economic calculation simultaneously. If an action is needed (e.g. aid application), the Gemini Agent opens the relevant government portal, pre-fills the form using your stored profile, and waits for your confirmation before submitting — keeping you in control at all times.
